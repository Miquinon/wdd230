Repetition

Walt Disney Education

WaltDisney.org

This site repeats the colors of orange, white, grey, and off blue. It also repeats the layout of photo and text next to each other in the desktop view.

Contrast

Business Software Solutions

WinWinIt.eu

This site uses contrasting colors and designs. Some of the edges are clean and cut while others are done with curvy edges. Dark photos are used against the light colored background.

Hierarchy

Hershey Land

HersheyLand.com

This site uses hierarchy to let you know what is important and leads you to what comes next. The titles are different from the subtitles and from the paragraph type. Each one is distinct in size and boldness.
