Membership Levels

Non-Profit Membership Benefits

- Access to networking events
- Listing in the chamber directory
- Educational workshops and seminars
- Advocacy and representation in local government

Bronze Membership Benefits

- All benefits of Non-Profit membership
- Enhanced listing in the chamber directory
- Access to member-to-member discount program
- Sponsorship opportunities at chamber events

Silver Membership Benefits

- All benefits of Bronze membership
- Priority placement in the chamber directory
- Additional promotion through the chamber's social media channels
- Invitations to exclusive member-only events

Gold Membership Benefits

- All benefits of Silver membership
- Exclusive access to leadership roundtable events
- VIP seating at Chamber events
- Featured article in chamber newsletter or website
